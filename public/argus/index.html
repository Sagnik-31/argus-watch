🔒 Proctoring Rules

  • Webcam & Microphone — Your camera and microphone will remain active throughout the exam for identity verification and audio monitoring.
  • Fullscreen Mode — The exam will run in fullscreen. Exiting fullscreen will be flagged as suspicious activity.
  • Eye Tracking — ARGUS tracks your gaze direction. Looking away from the screen for extended periods will be flagged.
  • Tab Switching — Switching tabs or windows is prohibited and will be automatically detected.
  • Copy/Paste Disabled — All clipboard operations are blocked during the exam.
  • Right-Click Disabled — Context menus are disabled throughout the exam.

🚨 How Flags Work

  • Face Mismatch — If a different face is detected, it will be flagged as a HIGH RISK event.
  • Audio Detection — Whispering, talking, or any suspicious audio above ambient noise levels will trigger a flag.
  • Multiple Faces — If more than one face appears on camera, it will be flagged immediately.
  • Suspicion Score — Each flag contributes to a suspicion score (0–100). Your session will be reviewed by an invigilator using AI analysis.
⚠️ All activity is recorded and will be reviewed. Any attempt to circumvent proctoring measures will result in automatic escalation.
